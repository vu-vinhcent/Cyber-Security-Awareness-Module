Project Abstract

Cybersecurity Awareness Module (CSAM) Project started as an interdisciplinary project where faculty and students from Computer Science and Psychology Departments collaborated to develop an interactive module to raise cybersecurity awareness among college students. The project was initially funded by the Interdisciplinary Initiatives Grant and the Department of Computer Science at Columbus State University in Columbus, GA in Spring 2016. The project is still being funded by the Department of Computer Science. A pre and post-survey instrument was developed and used along with the module to asses its effectiveness. Survey results from over 200 college students and over 100 high school students indicate a significant increase in students' awareness.
